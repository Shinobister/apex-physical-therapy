### **Project Master Prompt: Apex Physical Therapy Web System**

**Objective:** Develop a high-fidelity, responsive, multi-page web application for "Apex Physical Therapy" (Rocky Hill, CT). The site must be optimized for Chrome, Safari, and all mobile/tablet viewports using a "Mobile-First" development approach.

#### **1. Visual DNA & Design Language**
* **Aesthetic:** "High-Transparency Clinical Minimalism."
* **UI System:** Extensive use of **Glassmorphism**. Navigation bars and content cards must feature `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`, a semi-transparent white background `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.7)`, and a subtle `1px` solid border.
* **Color Palette:**
    * **Medical Cyan (`#00CED1`):** Primary action color (buttons, active icons, progress bars).
    * **Deep Slate Blue (`#2F4F4F`):** Primary typography and grounding elements.
    * **Soft Mint (`#E0F2F1`):** Subtle section backgrounds.
* **Assets Reference:**
    * *Hero Backdrop:* A wide, sunlit, minimalist clinic interior.
    * *Icons:* Minimalist teal line-art for Orthopedics (Joint), Sports (Runner), and Manual Therapy (Hands).
    * *Founder Portrait:* Professional headshot of Dr. Anand Bishnoi in a teal tie, integrated into the "Expert-Led" section.

---

#### **2. Page-by-Page Specifications**

**Page 1: The Landing Page (Conversion Engine)**
* **Hero Section:** Full-screen sunlit backdrop. Text: "RESTORE FUNCTIONAL INDEPENDENCE." Subtext: "Expert-led manual therapy and smart loading strategies in Rocky Hill, CT."
* **The "Three Pillars" Grid:** Uses the cyan icon set to describe Orthopedics, Sports Medicine, and Manual Therapy.
* **Philosophy Section:** Features a custom "Smart Loading" infographic showing the transition from injury to adaptive strength.
* **Founder Spotlight:** A "Meet your Doctor" card featuring Dr. Anand Bishnoi’s portrait with a glassmorphic overlay for his credentials.

**Page 2: Services & Conditions (Technical Depth)**
* **Interactive Accordions:** Detailed breakdown of treatments (Post-Op Rehab, Spine Care, Performance Training).
* **The "Smart Loading" Detail:** A dedicated deep-dive into the clinic’s evidence-based loading protocols using the progress-curve visual style.

**Page 3: Patient Portal & Resources (Utility)**
* **Resource Library:** Downloadable PDF links for intake forms using the UI Kit’s "Success Notification" style for download feedback.
* **FAQ:** Glassmorphic cards addressing insurance, direct access, and what to expect during the first visit.

**Page 4: Contact & Location (The Hook)**
* **Live Integration:** Functional contact form with real-time validation (UI Kit style).
* **Map:** Embedded Google Map pinpointing *825 Cromwell Ave, Suite Q, Rocky Hill, CT*.
* **Action Bar:** Sticky mobile footer with "Call Now" and "Book Appointment" buttons.

---

#### **3. Core Functionality & UX Requirements**
* **Sticky "Smart" Header:** The glassmorphic nav bar should shrink slightly on scroll and maintain the "Book Appointment" CTA at all times.
* **Performance Optimization:** * Lazy-load all high-resolution clinic imagery.
    * Ensure all transitions (hover states, page fades) occur over a `300ms` ease-in-out duration.
* **Interactivity:**
    * **Buttons:** Hovering over Medical Cyan buttons should trigger a "glow" effect or a slight scale-up (`1.05x`).
    * **Scroll Reveal:** Sections should fade in and slide up slightly as the user scrolls down the page.
* **Accessibility:** Maintain high contrast ratios for Deep Slate text against light backgrounds and include full ARIA labels for all interactive elements.
