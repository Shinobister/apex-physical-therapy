Build a high-performance, responsive website for **Apex Physical Therapy**. The site may be implemented as a single-page or multi-page experience using **React/Next.js** or **static HTML/CSS**.

# Objective
Create a polished clinical website that is visually aligned with the provided references, optimized for performance, and responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

# Design Direction
Adopt a **High-Transparency Clinical Minimalism** aesthetic.

## Visual Style
- Use **Glassmorphism** for the navigation bar and service cards.
- Apply the following glass treatment: `background: rgba(255,255,255,0.7)` and `backdrop-filter: blur(10px)`.
- Use a modern clinical sans-serif typeface such as **Inter** or **Poppins**.
- Feature a wide, sunlit clinic backdrop in the hero section.

# Technical Requirements
## Platform and Responsiveness
The site must be pixel-perfect and performance-optimized for:
- Google Chrome and Safari, latest versions
- Mobile phones on iOS and Android, using a **mobile-first** approach
- Tablets in both portrait and landscape orientations

  - Orthopedics
