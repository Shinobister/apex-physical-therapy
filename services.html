Orthopedic Physical Therapy

+

Comprehensive Musculoskeletal Care

Our orthopedic services focus on diagnosing and treating conditions affecting the musculoskeletal system—bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons.

Common Conditions Treated:
  • • Osteoarthritis & Rheumatoid Arthritis
  • • Rotator Cuff Injuries
  • • Knee Meniscus Tears
  • • Herniated Discs
  • • Plantar Fasciitis
  • • Tennis & Golfer's Elbow
Treatment Approaches:
  • • Progressive Loading Protocols
  • • Joint Mobilization Techniques
  • • Therapeutic Exercise Prescription
  • • Pain Management Strategies
  • • Postural Re-education
Typical Treatment Plan:

Initial evaluation (60 minutes) → Customized treatment plan → 2-3 sessions per week for 4-6 weeks → Progress reassessment → Home exercise program → Gradual discharge with maintenance plan.

Manual Therapy Techniques

+

Hands-On Healing Approach

Our manual therapy techniques involve skilled hand movements to improve tissue extensibility, increase range of motion, mobilize joints, and reduce pain.

Techniques We Use:
  • • Joint Mobilization (Grades I-V)
  • • Soft Tissue Massage
  • • Myofascial Release
  • • Strain-Counterstrain
  • • Trigger Point Therapy
  • • Dry Needling (Certified)
Conditions Benefited:
  • • Chronic Muscle Tension
  • • Joint Stiffness
  • • Scar Tissue Adhesions
  • • Nerve Entrapment
  • • Postural Dysfunction
  • • Headaches & TMJ
Treatment Progression:

Initial assessment of tissue quality → Gentle mobilization → Progressive deeper techniques → Integration with exercise → Education on self-management → Maintenance schedule.
